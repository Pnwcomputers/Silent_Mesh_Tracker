# 🕵️ Fox-Hunting: Manual Proximity Tracking

If cellular and GPS fail (underground garages), use the built-in **Private iBeacon**.

1. Download **"nRF Connect for Mobile"**.
2. Filter for UUID: `D34DBEEF-1234-5678-90AB-CDEF12345678`.
3. Walk the area. **RSSI** values closer to `-45 dBm` mean you are standing on top of the tracker.
